Korean pork belly

Description:

Shark fin soup is a traditional Chinese delicacy that has sparked significant controversy due to its impact on shark populations and ethical concerns. The soup is made by simmering shark fins, often with other ingredients such as chicken or broth, resulting in a gelatinous texture.

Ingredients:

  • 8 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • Chicken
  • 1 cup sliced shiitake mushrooms
  • 1 cup julienned carrots
  • 1 cup bamboo shoots, sliced into thin strips
  • 1/2 cup water chestnuts, sliced

Steps:

  1. In a pot, bring the chicken or vegetable broth to a simmer.
  2. Add shredded chicken (or tofu), shiitake mushrooms, carrots, bamboo shoots, water chestnuts, and corn to the pot. Simmer for about 10-15 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  3. Stir in soy sauce and sesame oil.
  4. Slowly pour in the cornstarch slurry, stirring continuously to thicken the soup.
  5. Gradually drizzle in the beaten eggs while stirring the soup in a circular motion to create silky egg ribbons.
